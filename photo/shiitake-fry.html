Visiting my friend Toshi in Rumford, Maine, where he grows mushrooms for his Shiitake Fry booth at the Common Ground Country Fair.

Toshi has been growing shiitake for over 50 years. When I visited him in April 2024, he showed me the early stages of the growing process.

In these photos, he is drilling boreholes into aspen logs before inoculating them with a pneumatic spore gun. Finally, he caps each hole with a styrofoam plug.

After that, he took me to his greenhouse, where the mushrooms spend a few weeks growing in ideal conditions.
